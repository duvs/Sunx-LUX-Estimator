SUNXCO PROJECT AGREEMENT AND DISCLOSURE

1. Agreement Overview
This Agreement is made between the Parties and may only be modified as specified herein. The contract documents shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida and are intended to be interpreted as mutually drafted. If any provision is found invalid, the remaining terms shall remain in effect. This Agreement supersedes all prior verbal and written discussions.

SunXco, through its approved dealer Epic Landscaping Inc., agrees to provide all labor and materials required for the project in accordance with the scope of work and specifications at the project site. All payments shall be made to Epic Landscaping Inc., the approved installer for SunXco pergolas.

2. Contract Documents
This Agreement encompasses all details of the project scope, additional agreements, written instructions, and approved change orders. In case of conflicts, this Agreement shall prevail. The Owner must review and approve shop drawings before permits or product orders proceed. Any delay in approval may impact the project schedule.

3. Payment Terms
Payments shall be made to Epic Landscaping Inc. according to the following schedule:
- 30% Deposit upon contract execution.
- 40%: (20% upon permit approval, 20% upon material delivery).
- 20% Upon pergola final completion.
- 10% Upon permit closure.

4. Design Revisions and Change Orders
Up to three (3) design revisions are included in the contract price. Any additional modifications or engineering fees exceeding $1,500 shall be the Owner’s responsibility. Changes to the scope or specifications shall be documented through a signed Change Order and may impact project costs and timelines.

5. Scheduling, Site Access, and Delivery
Epic Landscaping Inc. will make every reasonable effort to complete the project on schedule. However, permitting and regulatory approvals may affect the timeline. The Owner must ensure all preparatory work is completed as per approved shop drawings.

6. Warranty and Disclaimer
The SunXco pergola system is engineered to withstand various weather conditions but is not guaranteed to be 100% watertight under extreme rain or wind conditions.

7. Dispute Resolution
Before initiating legal action, Epic Landscaping Inc. shall have the right to address and remedy any construction defects. As per Florida law (Chapter 558, Florida Statutes), claims must go through a notice and cure process.

8. Insurance
The Owner shall maintain standard property and liability insurance. Epic Landscaping Inc. shall maintain general liability coverage with limits of $1,000,000 per occurrence and $2,000,000 aggregate.

9. Homeowner’s Association and Historic Approvals
The Owner is responsible for obtaining any necessary Homeowner’s Association (HOA) or historic district approvals.

10. Construction Liens
Under Florida’s Construction Lien Law (Sections 713.001 - 713.37), contractors and suppliers who are unpaid may file a lien against the property.

11. Modifications and Waivers
No modifications to this Agreement shall be valid unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

12. Limitation of Liability
Epic Landscaping Inc. and the Owner waive claims for consequential damages, including but not limited to loss of use, business revenue, or reputation damages.

13. Termination Policy
Due to the custom nature of SunXco pergola systems, orders cannot be canceled once placed. Payments made up to that point are non-refundable.

14. Cleanup Responsibility
Epic Landscaping Inc. shall maintain a clean and organized job site, ensuring all debris is removed upon project completion.

15. Electronic Signature
By signing below, both Parties acknowledge this Agreement as legally binding.
